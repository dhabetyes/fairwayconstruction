Locally Owned

Fairway Construction LLC was founded right here in Phoenix by a Valley resident of over three decades who started as an apprentice roofer and never left the trade. We've protected the homes and businesses of our neighbors — one roof at a time.

As a family-run company, we don't have regional managers or corporate quotas. When you call us, you speak directly to the owner. Every job gets the same attention to detail whether it's a small repair or a full commercial replacement.

We know the Phoenix climate — the brutal UV, the monsoon season, the extreme heat — and we choose materials and methods built to last in the Sonoran Desert.
